Before You Begin

Motor skills are how children use their bodies to move and interact with the world. This includes things like sitting, walking, running, climbing, using their hands, and picking up small objects.

Children develop at different rates, but identifying motor delays early can help your child get support sooner. This tool does not provide a diagnosis, but will help you create a list of concerns and observations to discuss with your pediatrician.
